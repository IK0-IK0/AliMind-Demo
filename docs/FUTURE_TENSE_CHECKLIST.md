## Notes for Conversion:
1. **Keep present tense for:**
   - Theoretical descriptions (TPB, TTM principles)
   - Mathematical formulas and definitions
   - General truths and established facts

2. **Convert to future tense:**
   - All methodology descriptions
   - System implementation details
   - Data collection procedures
   - Testing and evaluation plans
   - Development activities

3. **Watch for:**
   - Mixed tenses in the same paragraph
   - Passive voice constructions (was/were → will be)
   - Active voice constructions (did/does → will do)
